Online Sales Data Analysis

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an online sales dataset, aiming to uncover trends in customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and seasonality. I used various data visualization techniques to convey insights in a clear and actionable way.

Approach & Methodology

  • Data cleaning and preparation (handling missing data, normalization).
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to understand data patterns.
  • Created visualizations (pie charts, line graphs, histograms) to represent key metrics.
  • Identified trends such as peak sales periods and popular products.

Visualizations & Insights

From the analysis, I found that sales peaked during holiday seasons and that certain products consistently outperformed others. These insights can inform inventory and marketing strategies.
