Cluster Analysis on Framingham Heart Study Data

This project aimed to identify groups of patients with similar characteristics using cluster analysis. Using R, I analyzed the Framingham Heart Study dataset to find patterns that could reveal health risks across different clusters. Key steps included data cleaning, scaling variables, and choosing an optimal number of clusters through elbow and silhouette methods.

  • Tools: R, ggplot2
  • Skills Used: Clustering, Data Visualization
  • Outcome: Successfully identified distinct patient groups that may be at risk of getting heart disease and may require different preventive care approaches, aiding in targeted healthcare interventions.
